Shane McNulty

Irish Multidisciplinary Designer and Full Stack Web Developer with expertise in Branding, Web Design, Industrial Design and Web Development.

Hi im Shane McNulty and Welcome to my Portfolio Website. This Site is a compilation of some of my work to date within the realm of Creativity, Design and Web. The work included contains a mixture of Freelance, Employment and Educational Works.

I have over 4 years Experience in multiple area's of the Design Spectrum and has recently developed a strong understanding of both Front-End and Back-End Web Development.I Love my profession and love helping people who need creative and visual solutions in a world which unfortunately is plagued by poor design thinking.

Work Process

No Matter what the Job, There is a certain process i go through. This is to ensure the best end product for the client and the best form of communication for both client and myself

Discuss

1

Creative Concept

2

Production

3

Happy client

4

Meet with clients
Share with me you're idea's

Step 1 of any creative process.....you have a vision/idea, i need know what that is.

The Initial discussion can be considered he most important of a entire creative process

Creative Concept
Adding vision to you're Idea

After meeting with the client and terms have been agreed it's time to get to get the creative Jucies Flowing

No matter what the Project a series of concepts will be created, typically 2 -3, this allows the client to focus in on exactly what there vision is.

Production
Make it nice and shiny

When a concept or a combination of elements from different concepts have been agreed on by the client, its time to get it ready for the real world

Using the tools required the clients concept will be worked on in greater detail and ready for deployment and/or the next stage of its development.

Happy client
Im happy if your Happy

Once all the work has been completed and the client is happy. its time to package and or deploy

When terms are met and agreement between myself and client is fulfilled, All work is given to the the happy party
